Who We Are
✦

A Legacy Built on
Stone & Trust

Shree Salasar Stones was founded with a singular vision — to bring world-class marble and natural stone to the homes and businesses of Maharashtra at transparent, fair prices.

Established in 2005 at Balapur Kaman, Sambhajinagar, we began as a humble stone depot. Over two decades, we have grown into one of Maharashtra's most trusted names in the marble and stone trade, with a loyal customer base spanning the Marathwada region and beyond.

Our founder's belief was simple: every home deserves beautiful floors and surfaces, regardless of budget. That philosophy continues to guide every stone we source, every slab we cut, and every customer we serve.

Our Journey
✦

Milestones & Achievements

2005
Founded at Balapur Kaman

Shree Salasar Stone Depo opened its doors, offering quality marble and stone to the people of Sambhajinagar.

2010
Expanded to Italian Marble

We began importing premium Italian marble directly, bringing Carrara and Statuario grades to Maharashtra for the first time at accessible prices.

2016
New Showroom Opened

Our state-of-the-art showroom at Balapur Kaman opened — a destination for architects, interior designers, and homeowners across Marathwada.

2025
500+ Projects Completed

We proudly cross 500 completed installations — homes, hotels, offices, and heritage structures — across Maharashtra and beyond.
