Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide home delivery?

Yes, we deliver across Sambhajinagar and the Marathwada region. For bulk orders, we offer door delivery at competitive rates.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we have an experienced team of stone installers who can handle everything from flooring to wall cladding and countertop fitting.

Can I visit the showroom to see samples?

Absolutely! Our showroom at Balapur Kaman displays over 50 stone varieties. You can walk in any day from Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 7 PM.

Do you provide custom cutting and sizing?

Yes, we offer custom cutting, polishing, and edge finishing to your exact specifications. Bring your measurements and we'll handle the rest.
